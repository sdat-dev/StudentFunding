Finding the Right Funding Opportunity(ies) is Critical! Be sure to take the right steps to make sure you are optimizing your chances for success:

  • Search Funding Opportunities –Search the Student Grants Database under Awards & Funding (on the left navigation bar) to identify a funding opportunity that might be of interest. These funding opportunities are typically offered by federal and state agencies, private sponsors, and the University.
  • Good Fit – After finding a potential funding opportunity, the next step is to determine if it is a good fit for your. Carefully review the funding opportunity overview/guidance, eligibility criteria to ensure you are able to apply, the application process, and the key dates to make certain you can complete and submit the application on time.
  • Talk to a Mentor / Trusted Colleagues -You should talk with your faculty mentor, advisor and/or other trusted colleagues to discuss the funding opportunity and seek their thoughts and suggestions.
  • Contact the Sponsor – You should be sure to reach out to the program officer/manager of the funding opportunity to share your proposal concept, and inquire about guidance on the funding opportunity. Be sure to fully read the funding announcement beforehand.
