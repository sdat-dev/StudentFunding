Next you will need to define the scope of your project the scope of the project. keep in mind that your mentors, advisors, as well alumni and friend can help you achieve this step. Here are several questions to help guide you answering scoping and defining your proposed project.

  • What are the specific goals and objectives of my project?
  • What challenges am I seeking to overcome or address?
  • What is the scope and outline of my project?
  • What is the time-frame for the project and its activities, and will it be sufficient (particularly if the project involves other people and organizations)?
  • How does the project goals connect with my longer-term academic goals at UAlbany and career goals afterwards?
