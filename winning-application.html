Most of the information you will need about the UAlbany student funding opportunities available, their eligibility requirements, and their application processes and deadlines, can be found on the UAlbany Student Navigator. This is your first, and most important step, in identifying opportunities for which you and your proposed project may be eligible. If you're thinking of applying for an external fellowship, in addition to checking the Navigator, see the official website of that fellowship in order to determine your eligibility.
